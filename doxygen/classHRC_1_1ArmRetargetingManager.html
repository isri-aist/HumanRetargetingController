◆ CalibSource

using HRC::ArmRetargetingManager::CalibSource = std::unordered_map<std::string, std::array<sva::PTransformd, 2> >

Calibration source.

The format is {axis, {elbow pose, wrist pose}}. The axis is one of "X", "Y", or "Z". The elbow pose and wrist pose are expressed relative to the base pose.
